Digital System for Public Complaints and Resolution

Register Login Add a Problem Track Complaint Public Transparency

For Citizens

Citizens can register and report larger-scale civic and urban infrastructure problems, including roads, drainage, water supply, electricity, and sanitation systems, then track each complaint transparently from submission to resolution.

For Government Officials

Access submitted complaints, review issues, update the complaint status, and ensure timely resolution.

Tracking and Transparency

Each complaint is assigned a unique Complaint ID which can be used to track progress and update status easily.
